prototype 1

prototype A1 is is a time attack racer with an emphasis on speed and drifting. The goal is to stay as close as possible to the walls of the track to gain boost from the prototype's exhaust and drift around bends at neck break speeds! A dedicated site with more info, screenshots and videos is in development. The game itself will release first on steam sometime in 2021.
